INTRODUCTION

In the Calendar, all Work Order Management is displayed according to ther SO Deadline. Each card in the calendar represents a Work Order with its ticket number, priority, address and subject.

The calendar offers three views or layouts in the top right of the screen: Monthly,
Weekly, and Daily. By default, when you open the calendar, it will be set to Month.
